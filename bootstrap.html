Subject: National Pet Month

This month is National Cat/Dog Month! Did you know that visiting a vet at least once a year can help increase your pet’s life expectancy? Most pet parents only bring their pets in when they’re sick or injured. Instead, be more proactive and schedule your annual appointment.
